<p><p>The tennis shoes market is categorized into three main types based on court surfaces. Hard-court tennis shoes feature durable, non-marking soles for traction on rigid surfaces, providing stability and support. Clay-court tennis shoes have a herringbone tread pattern for optimal grip on clay, facilitating sliding and quick movements. Grass-court tennis shoes boast a lighter design with specialized studs to enhance traction on softer surfaces, allowing for agile play. Each type is tailored to the unique demands of its respective playing surface.</p></p>
