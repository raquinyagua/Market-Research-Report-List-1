<p><strong>The Sound Barrier Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective</li><li>Absorptive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Sound Barrier Systems market can be categorized into two primary types: reflective and absorptive systems. Reflective sound barriers are designed to deflect noise away from sensitive areas, utilizing materials that bounce sound waves back towards their source. In contrast, absorptive sound barriers aim to diminish sound energy by absorbing sound waves, often incorporating porous materials that reduce echo and overall noise levels. Both types serve essential roles in mitigating noise pollution in urban environments and around transportation infrastructure.</p></p>
