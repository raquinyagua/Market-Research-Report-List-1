<p><p>The light ice cream market features two main types: soft ice cream and hard ice cream. Hard ice cream, on the other hand, is firmer and has a denser texture, often served at lower temperatures. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers seeking indulgence with lower calories and fat content.</p></p>
