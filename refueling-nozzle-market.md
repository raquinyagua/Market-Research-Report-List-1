<p><p>The refueling nozzle market is divided into two main types: manual and automatic. Manual nozzles require operators to control the fuel flow, offering simplicity and lower initial costs, but they can be less efficient and pose safety risks due to human error. In contrast, automatic nozzles feature advanced mechanisms that detect when a tank is full, shutting off the flow to prevent spills. This type enhances safety and efficiency, making it a preferred choice for many modern fueling stations.</p></p>
