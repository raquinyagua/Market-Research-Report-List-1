<p><p>The malt ingredients market comprises three main types: dry extracts, liquid extracts, and malt flour. Dry extracts are dehydrated forms of malt, offering convenience and potency, making them ideal for various brewing and baking applications. Liquid extracts, on the other hand, provide a rich, concentrated source of malt flavor, commonly used in beer production and specialty foods. Malt flour, finely milled from malted grains, is primarily utilized in baking and culinary applications, imparting distinctive flavors and enhancing texture.</p></p>
