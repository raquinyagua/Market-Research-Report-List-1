<p><strong>The Large Size Panel Display Driver IC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Source Drive IC</li><li>Gate Drive IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Large Size Panel Display Driver IC market is categorized into two main types: Source Drive ICs and Gate Drive ICs. Source Drive ICs are responsible for controlling pixel brightness by supplying data signals to the pixel array, facilitating the color representation in displays. In contrast, Gate Drive ICs manage the timing and switching of the display's rows, ensuring that the correct data is presented sequentially. Together, these ICs are essential for high-quality image rendering in large panel displays.</p></p>
