<p><p>The Mechanical Individual Quick Freezing (IQF) equipment market primarily consists of Spiral IQF and Tunnel IQF systems. Spiral IQF equipment features a multi-layer conveyor that allows products to be continuously frozen in a compact footprint, ideal for processing large volumes efficiently. In contrast, Tunnel IQF systems use a long, straight conveyor with cold air circulation, effectively freezing products as they pass through. Both types ensure high-quality freezing by preserving texture, color, and nutritional value of various food items.</p></p>
