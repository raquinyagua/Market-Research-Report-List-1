<p><p>The semiconductor testing equipment market is categorized into two main types: testing equipment before packaging and testing equipment after packaging. Before packaging, testing equipment is used to evaluate die integrity and performance, ensuring chip functionality and quality. After packaging, testing equipment assesses the final product's performance and reliability, catching defects in the assembled device. Both phases are crucial for maintaining high standards in semiconductor manufacturing, minimizing failures, and ensuring the effectiveness of electronic components in various applications.</p></p>
