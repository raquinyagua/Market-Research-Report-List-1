<p><strong>The Indirect Acting Solenoid Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-Way Solenoid Valve</li><li>Three-Way Solenoid Valve</li><li>Four-Way Solenoid Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The indirect acting solenoid valve market comprises three main types: Two-Way, Three-Way, and Four-Way solenoid valves. Two-Way solenoid valves control flow between two ports, serving as simple on/off switches in systems. Three-Way solenoid valves manage flow routing between three ports, allowing for diverting flows or mixing applications. Four-Way solenoid valves, with their ability to control two separate outputs, are essential in pneumatic actuators and cylinder control. Each type plays a crucial role in automation and fluid control systems.</p></p>
