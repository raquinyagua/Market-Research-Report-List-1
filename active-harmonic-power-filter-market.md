<p><p>Active Harmonic Filters (AHFs) are advanced electrical devices designed to mitigate harmonic distortion in power systems. By dynamically monitoring voltage and current harmonics, these filters inject counteracting harmonics into the system, ensuring power quality and enhancing the efficiency of electrical networks. The demand for AHFs has surged due to the increasing reliance on non-linear loads, such as variable frequency drives and digital devices, which contribute to harmonic imbalance and affect the overall performance of electrical infrastructures.</p><p>The Active Harmonic Power Filter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period. <p><p>The Active Harmonic Filter (AHF) market includes Modular Active Power Filters (APF) and Wall-mounted APF systems. Modular APFs are designed for scalability, allowing users to add or remove modules based on changing power quality needs, making them ideal for larger industrial applications. Wall-mounted APFs, on the other hand, are compact solutions suitable for smaller installations or commercial spaces, providing effective harmonic mitigation without requiring extensive floor space. Both types enhance power quality, reduce energy losses, and improve equipment longevity.</p></p>
