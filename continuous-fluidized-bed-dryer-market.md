<p><p>The Continuous Fluidized Bed Dryer market primarily consists of two types: Fixed Type and Adjustable Type. Fixed Type dryers maintain a consistent bed height and airflow, ideal for uniform drying of homogeneous materials. In contrast, Adjustable Type dryers offer flexibility in bed height and airflow settings, catering to varying material properties and moisture levels. This adaptability allows for optimized drying processes, enhancing efficiency and product quality. Both types serve diverse industries, including pharmaceuticals, food processing, and chemicals.</p></p>
